How odds are built

  • Recent form: points per game and goal trends.
  • Goal model: Poisson scoring probabilities.
  • Blend: 70% goals model, 30% form adjustment.
  • Player impact: top scorers boost team strength.

This is a heuristic model for analysis. It is not betting advice.
